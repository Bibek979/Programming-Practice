Question 2: 

Given a positive integer n, do the following:

1. If 1<=n<=9, print the lowercase English word Corresponding to the number (e.g one for 1, two for 2).

2. if n>9, print greater than 9. 

Input Format    
-- A single integer, n.

Constraints
--1<=n<=10^9

Output Format
--If 1<=n<=9, then print the lowercase English word Corresponding to the number(e.g, one for 1, two for 2, etc.);
otherwise print  greater than 9.
